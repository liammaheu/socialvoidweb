about social void

Social Void is a pensive and lyrical alternative rock band based in Charleston, SC, fronted by brothers Chip Cooley (vocals, guitar) and Brent Cooley (bass, keys), along with Charles Holliday (drums). Their atmospheric debut album, Propeller, chronicles a deeply personal and prolonged time of isolation, in which Chip confronts his past, present, and future, and faces the consequences of disconnecting for too long. In former iterations of the band, they've become veterans of live performance and have recently added Quell Graham (guitar) and Elijah Kuzia (keys) for touring.
